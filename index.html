Indoor Plants

What Are Indoor Plants?

Indoor plants are species that thrive inside buildings. Most types grow in tropical and semi-tropical areas. They are happy with less sunlight and need less up keep than many outdoor plants. Common types include peace lilies, snake plants, spider plants, succulents, and pothos.
